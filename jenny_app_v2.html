Weight & Body 📊
Log daily — same time works best (morning, after bathroom)
Why track weight daily? One number means nothing. The trend over 2–4 weeks tells the real story. Expect normal fluctuation of 1–3 lb day to day from water, food timing, and your cycle. Don't react to a single number. React to the direction over time.

Nightly Check-in 🌙
A few honest questions. Takes 2 minutes.
Why this matters: You're starting HRT for the first time. Your body will respond over weeks, not days. These notes tell us what's actually working, what needs adjusting, and whether the doses are right. Be honest — even a bad day is useful data.
Tonight's Reflection —
How was your energy today overall?
Think about AM vs PM, during activity, and right now
How did you sleep last night?
Night sweats, waking up, racing thoughts, restless legs — note it
Mood today?
Anxiety, irritability, brain fog, or emotional swings — all worth noting
Any new sensations since starting HRT?
Breast tenderness, bloating, skin changes, spotting, headaches, nausea — all trackable, all normal early on
Hot flashes or night sweats today?
Tracking frequency helps us know if the estrogen dose is right
How was your digestion today?
Bloating, cramping, constipation, or loose stools — common early on with new supplements
Hunger and appetite today?
Unusually hungry, or no appetite at all? HRT and cycle phase affect this
Drive and motivation today?
Testosterone is partly for this — honest tracking helps calibrate the dose over time
Brain fog or mental clarity today?
Concentration, word-finding, memory — estrogen directly affects this
Any physical changes worth noting?
Skin texture, water retention, bloating, breast changes, hair — even subtle things
Anything else today worth noting?
Anything — pain, wins, questions, how you feel about all this. This space is just for you.
